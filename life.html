Life

Jonathan Meades was born in Salisbury, the offspring of a biscuit salesman and a primary school teacher. After a year at the University of Bordeaux training at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art he became a journalist and critic. He had been married 3 times and has 4 daughters.
